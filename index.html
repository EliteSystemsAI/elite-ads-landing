Before you spend

Common questions.

What budget do I need to get started?
The minimum useful ad budget is usually $500 per month. More competitive markets and service categories often need more signal to learn quickly. The free audit will show you a sensible starting range for your situation.
How does the AI optimisation actually work?
It monitors account patterns across search intent, keyword quality, competitor positioning, wasted spend, and conversion signals every day. The strategy and final decisions stay with the team. AI flags what needs attention so nothing drifts unnoticed between manual reviews.
Do I need to switch away from my current website?
Usually not. If the landing page is leaking leads, we'll call that out and recommend the simplest fix before asking you to raise spend. Sometimes a small change to a single page makes more difference than the ad account itself.
Are there lock-in contracts?
No. We keep clients through results, not paperwork. The setup investment covers the account build. From there, management continues month to month.
